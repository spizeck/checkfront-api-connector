# Travel & Concierge Information

## Getting to Saba

Winair Flight: - Shortest commercial runway in the world - Can fit 2--3
dives before last flight

Edge Ferry: - Tuesdays & Fridays - \$100 roundtrip

Makana Ferry also available.

Airport: \$10 cash Ferry: Included
