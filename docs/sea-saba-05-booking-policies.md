# Booking Rules & Policies

## Multi-Day Discount Policy

Guests may take one rest day during a multi-day dive package. More than
one gap day resets the multi-day discount.

## Sunset Cruise Minimum

Minimum 8 guests at \$50 each. If fewer than 8 guests: - Guests must
contact us to combine groups OR - Guests may pay for 8 to operate
privately

## Cancellation Policy

  Time Before Arrival   Refund
  --------------------- ----------------
  90+ days              100%
  45--90 days           75%
  30--45 days           50%
  Less than 30 days     Non-refundable

October, December, and January bookings are non-refundable.

Travel insurance strongly recommended.
