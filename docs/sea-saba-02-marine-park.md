# Saba National Marine Park & Environmental Policies

Established in 1987, the Saba National Marine Park protects Saba's
pristine marine ecosystem.

Coverage: - Entire island coastline - From high-water mark to 60m (200
ft) - Includes seabed and overlying waters

Managed by: - Saba Conservation Foundation

Funding: - \$3 Marine Park fee per dive - \$1 Hyperbaric Chamber fee per
dive (located in Fort Bay) - Donations & merchandise sales

Important Rules: - No fishing inside the Marine Park - Lionfish are the
only species allowed to be hunted - Lionfish hunting requires proper
certification

Sea Saba supports reef preservation through permanent moorings and
responsible operations.
