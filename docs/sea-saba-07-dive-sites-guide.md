# Dive Sites Guide

Saba Marine Park includes 30+ permanently moored dive sites within
minutes of Fort Bay Harbor.

## Pinnacles (FIRST DIVE ONLY)

Deep dives beginning at 80--110+ feet. Require strong buoyancy control
and proper nitrogen planning.

Sites include: - Third Encounter - Twilight Zone - Outer Limits - Shark
Shoals

Common marine life: Caribbean reef sharks, nurse sharks, schools of
jacks and creole wrasse.

## Mini-Pinnacles

-   Man O'War Shoals
-   Diamond Rock
-   Green Island

## Tent Bay Area

Vertical walls, garden eels, stingrays, excellent night diving.

## Ladder Bay Area

Spur and groove formations, turtles, nurse sharks.

## Windwardside

Weather dependent, white sand bottoms, elkhorn coral forests.

Site selection is based on daily conditions and diver experience level.
