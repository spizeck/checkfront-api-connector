# Sea Saba --- Brand & Company Overview

Founded in 1985, Sea Saba is the only continuously operating dive center
on the island of Saba in the Dutch Caribbean. Chad and Katy Nuttall took
over operations in May 2021.

Sea Saba operates inside the Saba National Marine Park and has built a
reputation for safety, professionalism, and small-group service.

We offer: - Daily diving excursions - Snorkeling trips - Discover Scuba
Diving experiences - SDI/TDI certification courses - Technical and
rebreather diving - Sunset cruises - Charter fishing (outside the Marine
Park) - Private charters and guiding

Core Values: - Safety first - Environmental stewardship - Professional
instruction - Transparent pricing - Personalized guest experience
